THE GAMES

A DEBT TO BE PAID

"We are playing a game, the host is allowing us to compete."

The Squid Game is a highly secretive, annual event created to provide entertainment for the ultra-wealthy. It recruits individuals from the edges of society—those deeply entrenched in debt and desperate for a final chance at financial freedom.

Based on traditional Korean children's games, the competition strips away social class and status, reducing all participants to players in green tracksuits. While the games appear innocent, the consequences of failure are permanent, fueling an ever-increasing prize pool.

For decades, the operation has been run in absolute secrecy on a remote island, overseen by the enigmatic **Front Man**. The history of the games is marked by desperation, betrayal, and a singular focus on the ultimate reward: **The Grand Prize.**

The choice is simple: risk your life for absolute freedom, or return to debt and despair.
